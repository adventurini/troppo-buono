Section 07 — Owner & Management

Bart Basilico

This is not a first-time restaurateur. Bart Basilico grew up inside a high-volume pizzeria, spent three years validating this exact concept on a food truck, and maintained that operation while working full-time at Ford Motor Company. The discipline required to do that is the same discipline that will run this restaurant.

Background & Track Record

1982
1982 — 2000
Salvatore's Pizzeria, Warren MI
Grew up working in his father's high-volume pizzeria. Learned dough production, cost control, and peak-volume kitchen operations firsthand. His father developed the original pizza techniques in Sicily before bringing them to the US and operating Salvatore's for 18 years.
FORD
Professional Career
Ford Motor Company — Full-Time
Maintained full-time corporate employment throughout the food truck years. Proof that the business success was not the result of undivided attention — it was built in the margins of a demanding day job.
2022
2022 — Present
Bart's Pizza Food Truck
Launched after a full year refining his proprietary dough formula. Served popular breweries, community festivals, and private events across Metro Detroit. Built loyal customers and proven commercial appeal before committing to brick-and-mortar.
2026
2026
Troppo Buono — Shelby Township
Permanent address. Full focus. Food truck scaled down as the restaurant opens. Owner present every operating day.

What the Track Record Proves

  • Product has genuine market demand — proven at real events with real customers paying real money
  • Dough formula and production systems are standardized and scalable
  • 100+ pizzas in a 4-hour window with a single oven — kitchen capacity is not a constraint
  • Operated profitably while holding full-time employment — operational discipline is built in
  • Brand identity and customer loyalty exist before the restaurant opens its doors
