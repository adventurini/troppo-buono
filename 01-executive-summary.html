Section 01 — Executive Summary

The Opportunity

Troppo Buono is a second-generation operator bringing a proven New York–style thin crust pizzeria concept to a high-visibility 4,043 SF building on Mound Road in Shelby Township, Michigan. We are seeking equity investment to cover the SBA 7(a) down payment and close this deal.

What We're Building

A full-service pizzeria and bar designed for predictable, high-frequency revenue. The concept combines a focused menu of thin crust pizza with high-margin bar offerings, takeout volume, and a disciplined operating schedule that keeps prime costs under 60%.

This is not a concept being tested for the first time. The product has been validated over three years of food truck operations at breweries, festivals, and private events. Brand identity, dough formula, and operational systems are established. We're putting them inside four walls on real estate we own.

Why This Works

  • Buying, not leasing. Property equity builds alongside operating income — two wealth engines, not one. No landlord risk.
  • Proven product. Three years of food truck operations demonstrate consistent customer demand before a single dollar of brick-and-mortar risk.
  • Lean model. Five-day dinner schedule. Thin crust means faster throughput and lower food cost. Break-even at $2,543/day — well below the $3,896 target.
  • Exceptional debt coverage. DSCR of 4.5 in Year 2. SBA minimum is 1.25. This business generates 4× the cash flow needed to service debt.
  • Existing infrastructure. Current restaurant use means kitchen, hood, fire suppression, and bar already in place — reducing buildout timeline and risk.
