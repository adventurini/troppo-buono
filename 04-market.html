Section 04 — Market & Location

Shelby Township

Mound Road in Shelby Township is one of Macomb County's highest-traffic commercial corridors — surrounded by dense residential neighborhoods, strong household income, and no direct NY-style thin crust competition at this quality level.

Location Advantages

  • High-visibility Mound Road frontage with abundant on-site parking — 1.59 acres
  • Dense residential base surrounding the property — strong repeat takeout demand
  • Affluent suburban demographic supports higher average ticket and bar revenue
  • Existing restaurant-use building with infrastructure reduces buildout timeline
  • Outdoor patio is competitive differentiation for warm-weather seasons
  • Adjacent to Utica/Shelby Township border — draws from two markets
  • No competing NY-style thin crust pizzeria with full bar in the immediate corridor
Market Upgrade vs. Original Concept

Shelby Township represents a market upgrade from the original Oak Park location — higher household income, greater traffic volume, and a larger 4,043 SF property with more seating capacity, a more robust bar, and outdoor patio. All increase revenue ceiling.
