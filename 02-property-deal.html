Section 02 — Property & Deal Structure

The Property

50285 Mound Road is a 4,043 SF freestanding commercial building on 1.59 acres in Shelby Township — fully equipped as a restaurant with existing kitchen infrastructure, bar, outdoor patio, and fire suppression. We are buying real estate, not signing a lease.

The property is currently operating as Esplendido Mexican Restaurant. Existing restaurant infrastructure — grease traps, hood systems, fire suppression, bar build-out — dramatically reduces buildout cost and timeline. We are starting with bones, not a blank slate.

Why Buying Beats Leasing

  • Property equity accumulates alongside operating income — two wealth-building engines, not one
  • No rent escalations, no lease renewal risk, no landlord leverage
  • Fixed debt service is predictable; occupancy cost locked for the 10-year loan term
  • Exit optionality: sell the business, sell the real estate, or both
  • Restaurant businesses sell at 2.5–3.5x EBITDA separate from real estate appreciation
